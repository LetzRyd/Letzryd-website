Your questions, answered

What are the Eligibility Criteria?

21+ Years of Age. You will need a Valid Drivers License, Aadhar & PAN Card, Local Address Proof to begin with registration.

Is there a deposit?

Yes, Our vehicles are accessible by payment of a nominal refundable deposit and a one time joining fee. The deposit varies depending on the type of the vehicle & city.

Should I have an existing account with Ride Hailing Apps?

No. We will onboard you on the apps without any charges.

When do I get paid?

Depending on the proposition payment are made on a weekly basis.

Are the vehicles insured?

Yes, All our vehicle are fully insured under comprehensive insurance policy.

Who maintains the vehicle?

LetzRyd maintains all its vehicles and the driver are expected to cooperate and attend scheduled services attend scheduled services, inspections as and when requested.
