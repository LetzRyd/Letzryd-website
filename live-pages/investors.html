Your Questions, Answered

What is LetzPartner programme?

The Partner program by LetzRyd is a vehicle lease alternate investment program. Under this, you the Partner buys and leases the vehicle to LetzRyd, for which you will receive a monthly assured lease rentals.

Who is eligible to participate in this programme?

Both individuals and corporates can participate in this. GST Registration is mandatory to participate (We can assist you in getting this)

What are the commercials?

The investment commitment is for a period of 4 years and the assured monthly Lease Rental depends on the vehicle and location.

Vehicle Level Concerns?

All repair and maintenance of the vehicle to be undertaken by LetzRyd and shall be responsible for any damage or on-road liability with regards to the car.

Vehicle Ownership Questions?

Partner has the flexibility of selling the vehicle at a pre-determined value to LetzRyd or retaining the vehicle. If a Partner chooses to opt out of the program before the end of tenor then it is subject to the terms of the lease agreement signed by both parties.
