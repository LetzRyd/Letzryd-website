LetzRyd in pact with MBSI

NEWS AND MEDIA

The Hans India

12/12/20241 min read

LetzRyd, a fleet management company, and Moto Business Service India Pvt Ltd (MBSI), a vehicle management provider, have announced a strategic partnership to deploy 51 Maruti Wagon R CNG vehicles in Bengaluru. The first batch of vehicles was handed over at a ceremony held at the LetzRyd Bengaluru Office. Tarun Jain, CEO, LetzRyd, said: “Today marks the beginning of an exciting chapter in our journey. Beyond addressing the urban commute needs of over 20,000 customers, we are also proud to be MBSI’s significant partner.”
